The Schedule

Tour departs at 11 AM from 802 Crossfit in Essex Junction with the first stop being Shacksbury Cider in Vergennes. About an hour will be spent at each location with the evening ending at Prohibition Pig in Waterbury for dinner. Some key items to remember:
North Branch closes at 5 PM.
Prohibition Pig does not take reservations or call waiting.
Stay longer if people are having fun.
Be responsible and don't forget to eat snacks!
